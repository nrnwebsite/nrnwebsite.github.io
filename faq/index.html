Frequently asked questions

Where can I get minecarts from?

    Many Minecart stations have chests with free minecarts. You can take one minecart from a chest. Remember to break the minecart after leaving the railway.
    Other than that, you can also obviously make your own minecart(s).

I've built a house/any other building but it is not connected to the railway. Can you please make a railway to the house/building?

    You can ask me to extend/build a railway line towards the direction of your house/any other building by contacting me via the contact page. Note that I do not guarantee that I will extend or build a new railway line.

I've found broken rails/corrupted chunks affecting the rails or minecart stations/partially or fully destroyed minecart stations, AFK pools or lava trash cans/broken or edited signs. How do I report that?

    Contact me via the contact page.

I've a question/suggestion/problem not shown here. How do I get in contact?

    Check the contact page.
